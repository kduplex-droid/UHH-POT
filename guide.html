HOW TO PLAY

Rules and guide for the UHH Terraria Click Guess Game

Back to Game

Game Idea

The goal of the game is to guess what the final click total for the day will be. Each player enters one guess before the guess window closes. During the day, the pot is clicked and the click total rises. At the end of the day, the player whose guess is closest to the real total wins that day.

How to Play

  1. Choose your character or select your name.
  2. Enter one guess for the day’s final click total.
  3. Guesses close at 9:10 AM or when clicking starts.
  4. The pot is clicked during the day to build the total.
  5. If there are accidental clicks, use the misclick box to remove them.
  6. At the end of the day, the closest guess wins.

Daily Rules

  • Each player gets only one guess per day.
  • A player cannot submit a second guess on the same day.
  • Guesses lock after 9:10 AM.
  • Guesses also lock as soon as clicking begins.
  • The player closest to the final click total wins the day.

Pot Rules

  • Each player puts in R2 per day.
  • There are 4 players, so the daily pot is R8.
  • The weekly pot for 5 days is R40.
  • The monthly pot grows based on how many active game days there are in the month.
  • The player with the most wins in the month wins the pot.

Leaderboards

  • Daily Leaderboard: total daily wins overall.
  • Weekly Leaderboard: wins during the current week.
  • Monthly Leaderboard: wins during the current month.

Misclick Rule

If accidental clicks happen, enter the number of wrong clicks in the misclick box. The system will remove those clicks from the daily total. Misclicks cannot be more than the current number of clicks for the day.

Winning the Pot

The main goal is to collect the most daily wins during the month. The monthly leader is the player with the highest number of wins. At the end of the month, that player wins the full pot.

Beginner Tips

  • Submit your guess early so you do not miss the 9:10 AM lock.
  • Watch patterns from previous daily results to make smarter guesses.
  • Check the leaderboard often so you know who is leading.
  • Report misclicks immediately so the total stays fair.
  • Use the weekly and monthly boards to track long-term performance.

Scoring System

  • The winner of the day gets 1 win.
  • Daily wins are added into the weekly leaderboard.
  • Weekly wins continue contributing to the monthly leaderboard.
  • The monthly leaderboard decides who wins the full pot.

Example: if William wins Monday and Wednesday, and Nickeel wins Tuesday, then William has 2 wins for that week and Nickeel has 1.

Example Round

Players and guesses:

  • William guesses 45
  • Rylan guesses 38
  • Nickeel guesses 51
  • Kgothatso guesses 42

Final click total: 44

Differences:

  • William: 1 away
  • Rylan: 6 away
  • Nickeel: 7 away
  • Kgothatso: 2 away

In this example, William wins the day because 45 is the closest guess to 44.
